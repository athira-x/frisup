Job Title : UI/UX Designer

Description

We are looking for a creative UI/UX Designer to design user-friendly and visually appealing
web and mobile interfaces. The role focuses on turning requirements into interactive designs
that deliver smooth user experiences.

Skills Required

  • Proficiency in Figma / Adobe XD
  • Wireframing & Prototyping
  • Responsive Web & Mobile Design
  • Basic understanding of Frontend (HTML/CSS)
  • Usability Testing & Iteration
